North Avenue Station

North Avenue station is an elevated Metro Rail Transit (MRT) station located on the MRT Line 3 system in Diliman, Quezon City. Located on the Epifanio de los Santos Avenue, it is named after the adjacent North Avenue, which intersects the highway. It is the current northern terminus of the line.

Nearby Attractions: Trinoma Mall, SM North EDSA
