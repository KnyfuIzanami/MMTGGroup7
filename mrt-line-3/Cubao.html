Cubao Station

Araneta Center–Cubao station, also known as Araneta–Cubao or simply as Cubao, rarely known as Araneta, is an elevated Metro Rail Transit (MRT) station located on the MRT Line 3 (MRT-3) system in Cubao, Quezon City.

Nearby Attractions: Araneta Coliseum, Farmers Plaza, Gateway Mall, SM Araneta City
