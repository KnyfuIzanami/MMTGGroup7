Batasan Station

Batasan Station, part of the MRT-7 line, is located in Quezon City near the House of Representatives. Nearby attractions include the Batasang Pambansa Complex, La Mesa Eco Park, San Mateo River Park, Ever Gotesco Commonwealth Mall, UP Diliman Campus, Quezon Memorial Circle, and Commonwealth Market. Transportation options include public transport, walking, or ride-hailing services. Ciudad Christhia Resort 9 Waves is a popular family-friendly resort in San Mateo, Rizal, known for its wave pool and recreational facilities. The resort offers day and night swimming, picnic and cabana rentals, function halls, and event venues. Visitors should book early, bring essentials, follow rules, and contact ahead for rates and availability.
