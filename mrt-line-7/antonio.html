Don Antonio Station

Tivoli Royale Country Club, Inc. is a premium recreational and lifestyle destination in Quezon City, offering a range of facilities for leisure, fitness, and social gatherings. The club features swimming pools, tennis and basketball courts, a well-equipped gym, on-site restaurants, and poolside dining. It also offers function rooms and banquet halls for events like weddings, birthdays, and corporate events. The spa services include massages, facials, and other wellness treatments. Blue Gardens Wedding and Events Venue is a picturesque location in Quezon City known for its romantic and elegant ambiance. It features European-inspired gardens, multiple indoor and covered outdoor venues, and elegant décor options. The venue offers comprehensive catering and event packages, customizable menus, and picturesque spaces for photography. Reservations are required for events and photoshoots, and inquiries can be made directly through the venue's office or website. Both venues are located along Commonwealth Avenue, Quezon City.
