Tala Station

Robinsons Supermarket at Metroplaza Mall in Caloocan City offers a convenient shopping destination with a variety of retail stores, a supermarket, and dining options. It's ideal for quick grocery runs and casual shopping. PILIPINAS is a nearby retail store offering a variety of items. LR Private Resort, located near Tala Station, offers swimming pools and event spaces for family gatherings or team outings. It's perfect for relaxation and private events. Both locations provide a peaceful getaway for gatherings, family outings, or unwinding in a scenic setting.
