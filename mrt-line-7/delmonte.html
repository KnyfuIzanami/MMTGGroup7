San Jose Del Monte Station

SM City San Jose del Monte, Vila Magenta Private Resort, and Avida Settings Altaraza Clubhouse are popular shopping and dining destinations with a variety of stores, entertainment, and dining options. The resort offers a serene setting for relaxation and unwinding, perfect for family gatherings, parties, or corporate events. Vila Magenta Private Resort & Events Place offers spacious outdoor areas, a pool, and event facilities, perfect for family gatherings. Avida Settings Altaraza Clubhouse, located in a masterplanned community, offers swimming pools, a basketball court, and landscaped common areas for relaxation and private gatherings. These places provide a blend of shopping, relaxation, and outdoor activities for a fun time with family or friends.
