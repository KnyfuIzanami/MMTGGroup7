Quirino Station

Yakal Coffee and Patio Isabel Swimming Pool are two popular relaxation spots in Quezon City. Yakal Coffee, located at the Million Trees Nursery and Eco Learning Center, offers a serene setting surrounded by lush greenery. Open Tuesday to Friday from 11:00 AM to 8:00 PM and on weekends from 8:00 AM to 9:00 PM. Patio Isabel Swimming Pool, a family-friendly resort in Sacred Heart Village, Novaliches, features two swimming pools: a lap pool and a children's pool. The entrance fee is ₱200 per person and operates from 8:00 AM to 5:00 PM. Both locations offer unique experiences for relaxation and leisure.
