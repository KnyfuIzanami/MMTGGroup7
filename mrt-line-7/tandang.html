Tandang Sora Station

The Tandang Sora Shrine in Quezon City, Philippines, is a historical and cultural landmark that honors Melchora Aquino, also known as "Tandang Sora," a key figure in the Philippine Revolution. A life-sized bronze statue of Aquino, surrounded by a landscaped park, provides historical information about her life and contributions. Aquino was a widowed mother who provided food, shelter, and medical assistance to the Katipunan, led by Andres Bonifacio. Despite her advanced age, she was arrested and exiled by Spanish authorities for aiding the revolutionaries. The shrine's peaceful garden setting offers visitors a peaceful space to learn and pay tribute to Aquino. Annually, on her birth anniversary, the shrine hosts commemorative ceremonies celebrating her legacy.
