Dona Carmen Station

Don Jose Heights Clubhouse and La Mesa Nature Reserve are two popular destinations near Quezon City. The Don Jose Heights Clubhouse is a community gathering venue with multi-purpose halls, swimming pools, and open spaces. It primarily serves residents but may accommodate external parties. The La Mesa Nature Reserve, located near the La Mesa Watershed, is one of the largest remaining rainforests in Metro Manila. It offers eco-tourism, conservation efforts, and guided nature walks. The resort's scenic views and picnic areas make it a popular destination for families and outdoor enthusiasts. Both locations provide a blend of urban convenience and natural beauty, catering to various recreational and community needs.
