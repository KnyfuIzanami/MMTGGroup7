University Avenue Station

The UP-Ayala Land Technohub Central Park is a serene and green area in Quezon City, offering a serene escape from the city's urban hustle. It features beautifully maintained lawns, gardens, and tree-lined paths, perfect for relaxation and outdoor activities. The park is surrounded by cafés, restaurants, and food stalls, offering a variety of dining options. A small artificial lagoon adds to the park's aesthetic appeal. The park is easily accessible for office workers, students, and residents, and is near attractions like UP Diliman Campus, Quezon Memorial Circle, and Commonwealth Market.
