Quezon Memorial Circle Station

Quezon Memorial Circle is a historical park in Quezon City, Metro Manila, Philippines, dedicated to President Manuel L. Quezon. The park features the iconic Quezon Memorial Shrine, a 66-meter tall mausoleum housing Quezon's remains. The park also features gardens, green spaces, and recreational activities such as biking and jogging paths, picnic areas, and fitness classes. Food and shopping can be found in a Tiangge (Bazaar) and food stands. The park hosts cultural performances, events, and environmental education through projects like the Joy of Urban Farming and the Aquarium and Aviary. The park is open daily from 5:00 AM to 10:00 PM and has free entrance fees.

Nearby Attractions: Ninoy Aquino Parks and Wildlife Center, Circle of Fun
