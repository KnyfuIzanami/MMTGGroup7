Balintawak Station

Balintawak Station is a stop on the Manila Light Rail Transit (LRT) Line 1, located in Quezon City, Philippines. Positioned along EDSA near the Balintawak area, the station is a vital transportation hub for commuters traveling to and from the northern parts of Metro Manila, including areas like Caloocan, Quezon City, and Valenzuela.
