Pedro Gil Station

Pedro Gil Station is a stop on the Manila Light Rail Transit (LRT) Line 1, located along Taft Avenue in Ermita, Manila, Philippines. It is named after Pedro Gil, a notable Filipino physician and public figure. The station serves as a vital access point for commuters traveling to and from various parts of the city, particularly those heading to the historic and cultural district of Ermita, as well as the bustling areas of Manila's southern districts.

Nearby Attractions: Robinsons Place Manila, National Museum of Fine Arts, Manila Doctor's Hospital
