Quirino Station

Quirino Station is a stop on the Manila Light Rail Transit (LRT) Line 1, located in the heart of Manila, Philippines. Positioned along Taft Avenue, it primarily serves the residents of nearby neighborhoods like Pasay, as well as commuters heading to and from major districts in Metro Manila. The station is named after Elpidio Quirino, the 6th president of the Philippines, and is strategically located near key commercial and educational areas.

Nearby Attractions: Roxas Boulevard, Manila Zoo, Malate Church
