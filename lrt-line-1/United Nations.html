United Nations Station

United Nations Station is a key stop on the Manila Light Rail Transit (LRT) Line 1, located along Taft Avenue in Manila, Philippines. Named after the United Nations, the station is strategically positioned near important government offices, embassies, and international organizations, making it a significant transit point for both local commuters and foreign nationals.

Nearby Attractions: San Agustin Church, National Museum Complex, Intramuros
