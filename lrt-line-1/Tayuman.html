Tayuman Station

Tayuman Station is a stop on the Manila Light Rail Transit (LRT) Line 1, located along Rizal Avenue in the Santa Cruz district of Manila, Philippines. Named after Tayuman Street, the station serves as an important transit point for commuters traveling to and from various residential, commercial, and industrial areas in Manila.

Nearby Attractions: Tondo, Santo Niño Church
