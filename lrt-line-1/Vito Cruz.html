Vito Cruz Station

Vito Cruz Station is a stop on the Manila Light Rail Transit (LRT) Line 1, located along Taft Avenue in the city of Manila, Philippines. It is named after Vito Cruz, a prominent Filipino educator and public servant. This station serves as a key transit point for commuters traveling through the busy areas of Manila and Makati.

Nearby Attractions: De La Salle University, DLSU Taft Avenue
