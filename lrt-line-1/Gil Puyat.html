Gil Puyat Station

Gil Puyat Station (formerly known as the Buendia Station) is a major stop on the Manila Light Rail Transit (LRT) Line 1, located in the heart of Makati City, Philippines. Situated along the busy Gil Puyat Avenue, the station is a key transit point for commuters traveling through one of Metro Manila’s most bustling business districts. It serves as a gateway to various commercial and residential areas in Makati, including the Makati Central Business District, making it an essential transportation hub for professionals and residents in the area.

Nearby Attractions: Cartimar Market, World Trade Center
