Roosevelt Station

Roosevelt Station is a stop on the Manila Light Rail Transit (LRT) Line 1, located in Quezon City, Philippines. Situated along Roosevelt Avenue, the station serves as the northern terminus of the LRT Line 1 and is a crucial transit hub for commuters traveling to and from the northernmost areas of Metro Manila, particularly in Quezon City and surrounding areas such as Caloocan and Valenzuela.

Nearby Attractions: UP Ayala Land TechnoHub, Diliman District
