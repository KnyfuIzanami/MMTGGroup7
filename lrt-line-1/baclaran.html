Baclaran Station

Baclaran Station is one of the busiest stations along the LRT Line 1, located in Parañaque, Metro Manila. It serves as a key transit hub for passengers traveling to and from various parts of the city, particularly those heading to the nearby Baclaran Church. The station is well-known for its proximity to Baclaran, a commercial and religious center, attracting both commuters and shoppers. Baclaran Station is equipped with basic amenities, including ticket vending machines, restrooms, and a large waiting area. It is a vital point for connecting with other modes of transportation, such as buses, jeepneys, and the nearby Paranaque Integrated Terminal Exchange (PITX).

Nearby Attractions: Baclaran Church, Victory Food Market
