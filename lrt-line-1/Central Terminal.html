Central Terminal Station

Central Terminal Station is a major stop on the Manila Light Rail Transit (LRT) Line 1, located in the heart of Manila, Philippines. It serves as a key transportation hub for commuters traveling to and from various parts of Metro Manila. Situated along Rizal Avenue, the station is in close proximity to important commercial, governmental, and historical landmarks, including Quiapo, Divisoria, and the bustling Manila City Hall.

Nearby Attractions: Manila City Hall, Liwasang Bonifacio (Bonifacio Park), Binondo
